About

Jerusalem is the capital of the State of Israel.
Jerusalem is a very special city where people live in a variety of ways and when it is important to the three major religions: Judaism, Islam and Christianity.
Jerusalem is full of tourist sites such as various museums, the Western Wall, various markets and other interesting sites. Furthermore, In Jerusalem there are historical sites that everyone must visit no matter what religion he is and let's not forget the nightlife in the city.

If you have not yet visited Jerusalem, you must come! A guaranteed experience for the whole family !!
